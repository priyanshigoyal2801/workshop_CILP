Overview

Why This Workshop?

As placement season approaches, many students find themselves technically skilled but unprepared for the real mechanics of recruitment. This workshop bridges that gap - bringing two engineers who cracked internships at Google and Salesforce to walk you through exactly what worked for them. Over two focused evenings, you'll build a stronger resume, sharpen your DSA skills, and leave with a clear, actionable strategy to pursue internships and placements with confidence.

📋
ATS-Optimized Resumes Learn how recruiters actually read your resume and how to pass automated screening systems.
🖋️
Off-Campus Strategy Discover how to find and apply for opportunities beyond campus drives - the way Pratham got to Google.
⚡
DSA for Interviews Topic-wise frequently asked questions with guided practice tailored to placement-level expectations.
🌐
LinkedIn & Visibility Optimize your profile so recruiters from companies like Google and Salesforce can find you.
